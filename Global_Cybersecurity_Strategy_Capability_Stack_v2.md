**Cybersecurity Strategy × CEO Leadership**  
Cybersecurity is the immune system of the digital enterprise. Every breach is a wound to trust. Every vulnerability is a bet against survival. Every security decision is a statement of values. A CEO with Cybersecurity mastery doesn't delegate IT security—they architect digital trust, cultivate security culture, and turn cybersecurity into a competitive weapon that assures customers, regulators, and partners.

**Synergy Thesis:**  
> *"Cybersecurity is not merely about firewalls and passwords. It is about trust architecture, digital resilience, threat anticipation, and the protection of everything that makes the enterprise valuable in a connected world. Master cybersecurity, and you master the art of digital survival."*
