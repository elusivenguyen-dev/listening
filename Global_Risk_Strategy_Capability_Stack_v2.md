## Executive Summary

The Global Risk Strategy (RSK) capability stack provides CEOs with a comprehensive 12-tier architecture for identifying, assessing, mitigating, and transforming enterprise risks across global operations. This capability moves beyond traditional risk management to strategic risk architecture — positioning risk as a competitive advantage and innovation catalyst rather than a defensive function.

**Strategic Imperative:** In an era of polycrisis, black swan events, and systemic volatility, the CEO who masters risk strategy transforms uncertainty into strategic optionality.
