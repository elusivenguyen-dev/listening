- **CM06-01 Executive Coaching at Scale** — CEO coaching, board chair mentoring, C-suite succession coaching, peer coaching circles
- **CM08-01 Trauma-Informed & Developmental Coaching** — Kegan developmental stages, adult development theory, psychological safety in deep coaching
- **CM11-01 Guru-Disciple & Socratic Wisdom Tradition** — Maieutics, dialectic, transmission beyond information, lineage and stewardship
- **CM12-01 AI-Augmented Personalized Development** — Adaptive learning paths, real-time feedback loops, digital twin coaching, predictive career modeling
