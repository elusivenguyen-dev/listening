## Điểm nổi bật

- **PA01-01 Relational Intuition & Trust Calibration** — Somatic sensing of partner reliability, embodied reciprocity, intuitive alliance potential, authentic connection as deal foundation
- **PA03-01 Partnership Strategy & Alliance Architecture** — Strategic alliance types (equity, non-equity, JV), partner selection criteria, governance structures, IP sharing, exit strategies
- **PA06-01 Ecosystem Strategy & Platform Partnerships** — Multi-party ecosystem orchestration, platform complementor strategy, coopetition, API partnerships, strategic alliance portfolio management
- **PA08-01 Cross-Sector & Public-Private Partnerships** — PPP models, NGO-corporate partnerships, academic-industry collaboration, open innovation alliances, consortium building
- **PA11-01 The Art of the Long Alliance — Trust as Capital** — Relationship capital accumulation, patience in partnership, covenant over contract, alliance as extended family, intergenerational partnership stewardship
- **PA12-01 AI-Powered Partner Intelligence & Ecosystem Optimization** — Predictive partner fit scoring, real-time alliance health monitoring, autonomous contract optimization, ecosystem value flow analysis, AI-mediated partnership formation
- **Master Formula PAE** = (Strategic_Alignment × Trust × Complementarity × Governance_Quality × Mutual_Value × Adaptability) / (Misalignment × Opportunism × Power_Imbalance × Bureaucracy × Short_Termism × Cultural_Clash)
