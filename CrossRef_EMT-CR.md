## Integration Philosophy

Conflict is fundamentally emotional. Traditional Conflict Resolution manages positions and interests. EMT-CR integration goes deeper to **analyze the emotional architecture of conflict** — the emotional triggers, emotional narratives, and emotional systems that sustain or resolve disputes.

> *"Conflict is not about what people want. It is about what people feel about what they want."*
