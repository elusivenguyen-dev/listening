## Master Formula — Decision Effectiveness (DE)

```
DE = (Quality × Speed × Stakeholder_Alignment) / (Bias × Regret × Cost)

Where:
- Quality = Accuracy × Completeness × Robustness (0-10)
- Speed = 1 / (Days from trigger to action) (normalized 0-10)
- Stakeholder_Alignment = % key stakeholders aligned (0-10)
- Bias = Detected cognitive bias count (inverse, 0-10)
- Regret = Anticipated regret score (inverse, 0-10)
- Cost = Resource cost / Budget (inverse, 0-10)

Target DE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
