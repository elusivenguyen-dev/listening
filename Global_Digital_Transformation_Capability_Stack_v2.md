- **DX01-01 Digital Mindset & Cognitive Flexibility** — Unlearning analog habits, embracing ambiguity, platform-first thinking
- **DX03-01 Technology Architecture & Cloud-Native Fundamentals** — Microservices, APIs, serverless, edge computing, SaaS/PaaS/IaaS literacy
- **DX06-01 Enterprise Digital Transformation Strategy** — McKinsey 7S for digital, bimodal IT, platform business models, CIO-CEO alignment
- **DX08-01 Legacy Modernization & Technical Debt Management** — Strangler fig pattern, refactoring at scale, brownfield vs. greenfield decisions
- **DX11-01 Dao of Digital — Technology as Extension of Human Potential** — Tools serving wisdom, digital minimalism, intentionality over automation
- **DX12-01 Self-Optimizing Digital Enterprise** — Autonomous operations, self-healing systems, AI-driven process discovery and reengineering
- **Master Formula DXE** = (Vision × Architecture × Adoption × Agility × Ecosystem_Integration) / (Legacy_Inertia × Silo_Thinking × Hype_Driven × Change_Resistance × Technical_Debt)
