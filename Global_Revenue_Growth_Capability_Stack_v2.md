## Điểm nổi bật

- **RG01-01 Growth Mindset & Abundance Psychology** — Somatic expansion, comfort with scale, rejection resilience, embodied confidence in value creation
- **RG03-01 Growth Strategy & Revenue Architecture** — Ansoff matrix, BCG growth-share, SaaS metrics (ARR, MRR, CAC, LTV), pricing strategy, go-to-market models
- **RG06-01 Board-Level Growth Governance & Capital Allocation for Growth** — Growth vs. profitability trade-offs, R&D investment discipline, market expansion decisions, organic vs. inorganic growth
- **RG08-01 Hypergrowth & Scale Economics** — Blitzscaling, network effects, viral loops, marketplace liquidity, platform monetization, international expansion, emerging market penetration
- **RG11-01 Sustainable Growth & Anti-Fragile Enterprise** — Growth that strengthens, Taleb's anti-fragility, compounding over hype, quality revenue over vanity growth, growth as byproduct of excellence
- **RG12-01 AI-Powered Growth Engine & Predictive Revenue Optimization** — AI sales forecasting, autonomous pricing, predictive churn prevention, generative lead creation, real-time revenue optimization
- **Master Formula RGE** = (Market_Opportunity × Value_Proposition × Go_To_Market_Efficiency × Retention × Expansion × Pricing_Power) / (Churn × Acquisition_Cost_Inflation × Market_Misfit × Premature_Scaling × Unsustainable_Discounting)
