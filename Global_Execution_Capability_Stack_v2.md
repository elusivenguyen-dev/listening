## Master Formula — Execution Effectiveness (EE)

```
EE = (Speed × Quality × Reliability × Sustainability) / (Rework × Burnout × Hero_Dependency)

Where:
- Speed = Time to completion vs. plan (0-10)
- Quality = Defect rate, acceptance criteria met (0-10)
- Reliability = Consistency of delivery (0-10)
- Sustainability = Energy preserved for long-term (0-10)
- Rework = % work requiring redo (inverse, 0-10)
- Burnout = Team energy depletion (inverse, 0-10)
- Hero_Dependency = Reliance on individual heroes (inverse, 0-10)

Target EE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
