## Integration Philosophy

70% of change initiatives fail — largely because they ignore emotions. Change Management without Emotion Thinking is **process without people**. Emotion Thinking without Change Management is **insight without action**. The EMT-CM integration ensures that every change journey is **emotionally mapped, emotionally managed, and emotionally completed**.

> *"People do not resist change. They resist the emotional experience of change."*
