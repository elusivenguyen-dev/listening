## Total Executive Mastery Index (TEM) v6.0

### Formula

```
TEM = 13√(LE × RE × WE × SE × DME × STE × EIE × NE × EE × IE × PFE × FIE × NBE) × PD × AI × WI

Where:
- LE = Listening Effectiveness (0-10)
- RE = Reading Effectiveness (0-10)
- WE = Writing Effectiveness (0-10)
- SE = Speaking Effectiveness (0-10)
- DME = Decision Making Effectiveness (0-10)
- STE = Systems Thinking Effectiveness (0-10)
- EIE = Emotional Intelligence Effectiveness (0-10)
- NE = Negotiation Effectiveness (0-10)
- EE = Execution Effectiveness (0-10)
- IE = Innovation Effectiveness (0-10)
- PFE = Physical Fitness Effectiveness (0-10)
- FIE = Financial Intelligence Effectiveness (0-10)
- NBE = Network Building Effectiveness (0-10)
- PD = Processing Depth (0-10) — integration quality across capabilities
- AI = AI Amplification (0-10) — AI augmentation level
- WI = Wisdom Integration (0-10) — long-term, ethical, institutional integration

Target:
- Manager: TEM > 10.0
- Director: TEM > 20.0
- VP: TEM > 35.0
- CEO: TEM > 50.0
- Institution Builder: TEM > 100.0
- AI-Augmented Leader: TEM > 150.0
```
