## Master Formula — Network Building Effectiveness (NBE)

```
NBE = (Diversity × Depth × Reciprocity × Strategic_Alignment × Generosity) / (Transactionality × Superficiality × Burnout)

Where:
- Diversity = Cross-industry, cross-seniority, cross-geography (0-10)
- Depth = Average relationship quality (0-10)
- Reciprocity = Balance of giving/receiving (0-10)
- Strategic_Alignment = Network supports business goals (0-10)
- Generosity = Introductions made, help offered (0-10)
- Transactionality = Asking without giving (inverse, 0-10)
- Superficiality = Connections without substance (inverse, 0-10)
- Burnout = Social exhaustion (inverse, 0-10)

Target NBE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
