## Integration Philosophy

**Real Estate Strategy × CEO Leadership**  
Real estate is the physical manifestation of organizational strategy. Every square foot is a statement of priority. Every location decision is a bet on the future. Every building is a cultural artifact. A CEO with Real Estate Strategy mastery doesn't lease space—they architect environments that amplify mission, attract talent, and compound value through market cycles.

**Synergy Thesis:**  
> *"Real estate is the only asset class that is simultaneously: a balance sheet item, an operating expense, a talent magnet, a brand statement, a community anchor, and a generational wealth creator. Master it, and you master the physical dimension of enterprise value."*

- RES × FI (Financial Intelligence): Capital allocation, REIT structuring, valuation mastery
