## Master Formula — RSTE (Resilience Thinking Effectiveness)

```
RSTE = (Absorptive_Capacity × Adaptive_Capacity × Transformative_Capacity × Redundancy_Design × Diversity_Leverage × Modularity_Architecture × Feedback_Responsiveness × Learning_Speed × Recovery_Velocity × AI_Augmentation)
       / (Fragility × Rigidity × Homogeneity × Centralization × Slowness × Complacency × Blindness × Denial × Inaction × Fragile_Efficiency)
```

**Target Scores:**
- CEO Practitioner: RSTE > 750
- Resilient Organization: RSTE > 1500
- Anti-Fragile System: RSTE > 3000
