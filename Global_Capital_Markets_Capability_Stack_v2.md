## Điểm nổi bật

- **CM01-01 Market Instinct & Capital Intuition** — Somatic sensing of market sentiment, embodied risk-reward calibration, intuitive timing for capital decisions, gut feeling for liquidity
- **CM03-01 Capital Markets Fundamentals & Instruments** — Equity markets, debt markets, derivatives, FX, commodities, structured products, SPACs, IPO process, secondary offerings, private placements
- **CM06-01 CFO & Board Capital Strategy** — Capital structure optimization, cost of capital management, dividend policy, share buybacks, access to capital markets, rating agency management
- **CM08-01 Global Capital Raising & Complex Transactions** — Cross-border listings (ADR/GDR), sovereign wealth fund engagement, private equity co-investment, venture debt, convertible instruments, green bonds, Islamic finance
- **CM11-01 Capital as Trust — Long-Term Market Stewardship** — Patient capital, long-term investing philosophy, Buffett capital allocation wisdom, avoiding short-term market pressure, capital as servant of mission
- **CM12-01 AI-Powered Capital Markets & Algorithmic Finance** — Automated trading strategies, predictive capital raising timing, AI credit scoring, DeFi integration, tokenized securities, real-time capital optimization
- **Master Formula CME** = (Access × Cost_Efficiency × Timing × Flexibility × Investor_Relations × Strategic_Alignment) / (Market_Volatility × Short_Termism × Dilution × Regulatory_Barriers × Liquidity_Crunch)
