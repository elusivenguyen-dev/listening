## Integration Philosophy

**Product Strategy × CEO Leadership**  
Product is the physical manifestation of strategy. Every feature is a bet on customer need. Every roadmap is a vision of the future. Every product decision is a statement of values. A CEO with Product Strategy mastery doesn't delegate roadmaps—they architect product-market fit, cultivate innovation velocity, and turn product into the ultimate competitive weapon that creates customers, not just captures them.

**Synergy Thesis:**  
> *"Product is not merely what you build. Product is the promise you make, the problem you solve, the experience you deliver, and the relationship you cultivate. Master product strategy, and you master the art of creating value that customers didn't know they needed—and cannot live without."*

**Cross-Capability Architecture:**
- PRS × IN (Innovation): R&D, design thinking, prototyping, experimentation, breakthrough innovation
- PRS × MA (Market Analysis): Market sizing, segmentation, competitive analysis, trend forecasting
- PRS × STG (Strategic Thinking): Portfolio strategy, platform strategy, ecosystem design, market entry
