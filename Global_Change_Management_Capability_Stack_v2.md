## Điểm nổi bật

- **CH01-01 Personal Change Resilience & Adaptability** — Somatic flexibility, comfort with ambiguity, personal transition curve awareness, change as growth mindset
- **CH03-01 Change Management Models & Methodologies** — Kotter 8-step, ADKAR, Prosci, Lewin change model, Bridges transitions, appreciative inquiry
- **CH06-01 Enterprise Transformation & Cultural Change** — Large-scale transformation office, change leadership at board level, M&A cultural integration, digital transformation change
- **CH08-01 Change in High-Stakes & Regulated Environments** — Change in healthcare, financial services, government, military, crisis-driven change, turnaround management
- **CH11-01 Impermanence as Wisdom — Embracing Flux** — Buddhist anicca, Heraclitus, organizational entropy, change as the only constant, leading through paradox
- **CH12-01 AI-Augmented Continuous Organizational Evolution** — Real-time organizational sensing, predictive change readiness, automated nudge interventions, digital twin organization modeling
