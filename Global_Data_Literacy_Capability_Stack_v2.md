## Điểm nổi bật

- **DL03-01 Statistical Thinking & Probability Literacy** — Base rates, Bayesian updating, confidence intervals, correlation vs. causation
- **DL04-01 Data Visualization & Narrative** — Tufte principles, cognitive load, truthfulness, chartjunk elimination
- **DL06-01 Executive Data Governance & Monetization** — Data as strategic asset, CDO role, data ethics board, privacy by design
- **DL08-01 Advanced Analytics & Machine Learning Interpretability** — SHAP, LIME, causal inference, experiment design
- **DL11-01 Epistemic Humility & Data Wisdom** — Knowing the limits of knowing, missing data, unknown unknowns, OODA loops
- **DL12-01 Autonomous Insight Generation** — AI agents discovering patterns, natural language queries, real-time anomaly detection
- **Master Formula DLE** = (Collection_Quality × Analytical_Rigor × Interpretation_Accuracy × Communication_Clarity × Ethical_Guardrails) / (Confirmation_Bias × Overfitting × Misrepresentation × Data_Silos × Surveillance_Culture)
