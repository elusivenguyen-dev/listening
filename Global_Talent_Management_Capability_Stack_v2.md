## Điểm nổi bật

- **TLM01-01 Energetic Awareness of Talent** — Reading human potential, somatic attunement to team dynamics, intuitive talent spotting, embodied leadership assessment
- **TLM03-01 Talent Science & People Analytics** — Psychometrics, structured interviewing, predictive hiring, performance calibration, 9-box grid, talent reviews, bias mitigation
- **TLM06-01 Executive Talent Strategy** — Succession planning, C-suite pipeline, board composition, high-potential identification, retention of critical talent, war for talent
- **TLM08-01 Global Talent Mobility & Remote-First Organization** — Distributed team design, global compensation, visa/immigration strategy, async culture, digital nomad policies
- **TLM11-01 Cultivating Human Potential as Legacy** — Mentorship ecosystems, long-term talent stewardship, organizational memory, wisdom transfer, human-centric capitalism
- **TLM12-01 AI-Augmented Talent Ecosystem** — AI recruiting, skills inference, internal mobility AI, predictive flight risk, personalized development paths, talent marketplace algorithms
- **Master Formula TLME** = (Acquisition_Quality × Development_Depth × Retention_Rate × Diversity × Cultural_Alignment × Potential_Realization) / (Bias × Bureaucracy × Nepotism × Stagnation × Short_Term_Hiring)
