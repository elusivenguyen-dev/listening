## Điểm nổi bật

- **CX01-01 Embodied Customer Empathy** — Walking in customer's shoes, somatic resonance with user frustration, mirror neuron activation in service contexts
- **CX03-01 Customer Experience Frameworks & Metrics** — NPS, CES, CSAT, journey mapping, service blueprinting, voice-of-customer (VoC) programs
- **CX06-01 Board-Level CX Strategy & Customer-Centric Transformation** — CX as board priority, customer equity, lifetime value governance, CCO role, CX maturity model
- **CX08-01 Omnichannel Experience Architecture & Behavioral Economics** — Channel orchestration, friction elimination, choice architecture, nudge theory in CX, anticipatory service
- **CX11-01 The Guest as Sacred — Hospitality Wisdom Across Cultures** — Omotenashi, ubuntu in service, treating strangers as honored guests, service as spiritual practice
- **CX12-01 AI-Powered Predictive & Emotion-Aware Experience** — Real-time sentiment analysis, predictive next-best-action, emotion AI, autonomous experience optimization, hyper-personalization at scale
- **Master Formula CXE** = (Empathy × Consistency × Personalization × Resolution_Speed × Surprise_Delight × Trust) / (Friction × Inconsistency × Automation_Coldness × Data_Creep × Complacency)
