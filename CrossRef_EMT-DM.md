## Integration Philosophy

Traditional Decision Making treats emotions as **noise to be eliminated**. Behavioral economics treats emotions as **biases to be managed**. Emotion Thinking treats emotions as **data to be integrated**. The EMT-DM integration makes every decision **emotionally informed** — not emotionally dominated, but emotionally complete.

> *"The best decisions are not emotionless. They are emotionally aware."*
