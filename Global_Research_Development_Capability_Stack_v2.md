## Điểm nổi bật

- **RD01-01 Curiosity as Physiological Drive** — Embodied wonder, somatic openness to discovery, personal research discipline, intellectual hunger as vital force
- **RD03-01 R&D Methodologies & Innovation Funnel** — Stage-gate, TRIZ, design of experiments, agile R&D, open innovation, technology readiness levels (TRL), portfolio management
- **RD06-01 Board-Level R&D Strategy & Capital Allocation** — R&D intensity benchmarking, basic vs. applied research balance, R&D ROI measurement, moonshot programs, corporate venturing
- **RD08-01 Advanced R&D Ecosystems & Scientific Collaboration** — National lab partnerships, university research alliances, CRO management, clinical trial design, regulatory science, frontier technology scouting
- **RD11-01 The Long Now of Research — Patience & Perseverance** — Basic research as sacred trust, long-term scientific commitment, failure as learning, research integrity, knowledge for its own sake
- **RD12-01 AI-Accelerated Discovery & Autonomous Research** — Generative molecular design, automated hypothesis generation, AI lab assistant, self-driving labs, predictive experiment design, knowledge graph discovery
- **Master Formula RDE** = (Curiosity × Methodology × Investment × Collaboration × Speed_to_Insight × Translation) / (Bureaucracy × Silo_Research × Short_Termism × Publication_Bias × Resource_Waste × Ivory_Tower)
