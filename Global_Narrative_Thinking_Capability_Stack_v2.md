## Master Formula — NATE (Narrative Thinking Effectiveness)

```
NATE = (Story_Awareness × Narrative_Craft × Meaning_Making × Stakeholder_Resonance × Cultural_Fit × Temporal_Depth × Strategic_Alignment × Emotional_Impact × Identity_Architecture × AI_Augmentation)
       / (Narrative_Blindness × Storytelling_Without_Strategy × Cultural_Mismatch × Shallow_Narratives × Inauthenticity × Temporal_Myopia × Misalignment × Emotional_Manipulation × Identity_Crisis × Inaction)
```

**Target Scores:**
- CEO Practitioner: NATE > 750
- Narrative-Driven Organization: NATE > 1500
- AI-Augmented Narrative System: NATE > 3000
