## 10. KPI & Observability

### Individual Level
- **Attention Quality:** % of conversations with full presence (self-assessed)
- **Clarification Rate:** Questions asked per conversation
- **Paraphrase Accuracy:** Feedback on understanding accuracy

### Organizational Level
- **Listening Coverage:** % of stakeholder segments with active listening channels
- **Signal Detection:** Weak signals captured and validated per quarter
- **Decision Quality:** Post-decision review scores linked to listening inputs

### System Level
- **Learning Velocity:** Time from signal to organizational response
- **Adaptive Capacity:** % of assumptions revised per quarter
- **Institutional Trust:** Long-term stakeholder trust metrics
