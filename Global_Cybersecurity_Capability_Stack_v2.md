## Điểm nổi bật

- **CY01-01 Security Awareness as Second Nature** — Phishing resistance, password hygiene, physical security, social engineering intuition
- **CY03-01 NIST & Zero Trust Architecture** — Identify, protect, detect, respond, recover; never trust, always verify; least privilege
- **CY06-01 Board-Level Cyber Risk Governance** — CISO reporting lines, cyber insurance, incident response at board level, regulatory compliance (DORA, NIS2)
- **CY08-01 Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) & Nation-State Defense** — Threat intelligence, attribution, supply chain attacks, cyber warfare implications
- **CY11-01 The Guardian Ethic — Stewardship of Trust** — Security as sacred duty, privacy as human right, restraint in surveillance power
- **CY12-01 AI-Powered Autonomous Security Operations** — Self-defending networks, predictive threat hunting, AI vs. AI cyber warfare, quantum-resistant cryptography
- **Master Formula CYE** = (Prevention × Detection × Response × Recovery × Resilience × Trust) / (Complacency × Complexity × Silo_Security × Insider_Threat × Attack_Surface)
