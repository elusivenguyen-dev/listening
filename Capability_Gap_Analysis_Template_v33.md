## 📋 Template Overview

This template provides a structured framework for:
- **Identifying** capability gaps at individual, team, and enterprise levels
- **Prioritizing** gaps based on strategic importance and urgency
- **Quantifying** the impact of gaps on business outcomes
- **Planning** targeted development interventions
- **Tracking** closure progress over time
