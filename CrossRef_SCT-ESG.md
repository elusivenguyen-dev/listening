## Integration Philosophy

ESG without Scenario Thinking is **compliance with today's standards**. Scenario Thinking without ESG is **futures without responsibility**. The SCT-ESG integration ensures that sustainability is **future-proof** and that scenarios include **planetary and social boundaries**.

> *"Sustainability is not a destination. It is a trajectory through multiple possible futures."*

## Joint Programs (12 Integrated Programs)

### SCT-ESG-01: NGFS Scenario Integration
**Description:** Integrate NGFS (Network for Greening the Financial System) climate scenarios into enterprise strategy.  
**Method:** NGFS scenarios × enterprise risk/strategy  
**Output:** NGFS-aligned enterprise strategy

### SCT-ESG-02: TCFD Scenario Disclosure
**Description:** Build TCFD-aligned scenario analysis for climate disclosure.  
**Method:** TCFD framework × scenario methodology  
**Output:** TCFD-compliant scenario disclosure

### SCT-ESG-03: Transition Pathway Scenario Design
**Description:** Design multiple transition pathways ( orderly, disorderly, hot house world).
