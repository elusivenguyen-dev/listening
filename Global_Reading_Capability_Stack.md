## 9. Learning Progression Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1–3)
- **L1:** Speed reading / PhotoReading fundamentals
- **L2:** Active reading + SQ3R + Cornell Notes mastery
- **L3:** Academic reading basics (parse 1 paper/week)

### Phase 2: Analytical (Months 4–6)
- **L4:** Argument analysis (map 10 arguments/week)
- **L5:** Critical evaluation (source credibility audit)

### Phase 3: Executive (Months 7–9)
- **L6:** Strategic reading (HBR, McKinsey, 10-Ks)
- **L7:** Cross-disciplinary reading (systems + history + economics)

### Phase 4: Expert (Months 10–12)
- **L8:** Deep domain mastery (research synthesis)
- **L9:** Leadership/institutional reading (board papers, policy)

### Phase 5: Creation (Months 13–18)
- **L10:** Reading-to-create (write essays, design frameworks)
- **L11:** Wisdom reading (Great Books, philosophy, civilizational history)

### Phase 6: Adaptive (Months 19–24)
- **L12:** AI-augmented reading (LLM synthesis, knowledge graphs)
- Integration and teaching

## 10. KPI & Observability

### Individual Level
- **Reading Velocity:** Words per minute (WPM) with comprehension check
- **Retention Rate:** 24-hour recall accuracy (self-tested)
- **Synthesis Rate:** Notes-to-pages ratio

### Organizational Level
- **Reading Coverage:** % of strategic documents read by leadership
- **Insight Extraction:** Actionable insights per document
- **Decision Linkage:** % of decisions traceable to reading inputs

### System Level
- **Knowledge Reuse:** % of reading notes reused in outputs
- **Cross-Disciplinary Integration:** Connections made across domains per quarter
- **Institutional Memory:** Long-term knowledge persistence rate
