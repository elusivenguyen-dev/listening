## Integration Philosophy

Traditional Risk Management asks: *"What could go wrong?"* Scenario Thinking asks: *"What futures could unfold?"* The SCT-RM integration replaces **backward-looking risk lists** with **forward-looking risk scenarios**, transforming risk from a compliance exercise into a strategic foresight function.

> *"Risk is not a list. Risk is a landscape of possible futures."*
