## Điểm nổi bật

- **MA01-01 M&A Intuition & Somatic Deal Sense** — Gut feeling calibration, body-based risk sensing, emotional regulation during high-stakes negotiations
- **MA03-01 M&A Fundamentals & Transaction Architecture** — Valuation methods (DCF, comparable, precedent), deal structures (asset vs. stock, merger, JV), due diligence frameworks
- **MA06-01 M&A Integration Strategy** — Post-merger integration (PMI), synergy capture, culture integration, retention strategy, 100-day plan
- **MA08-01 Cross-Border M&A & Regulatory Navigation** — CFIUS, antitrust (HSR), EU merger control, emerging market entry, tax optimization
- **MA11-01 The Art of the Long-Term Deal** — Buffett acquisition philosophy, permanent capital, cultural fit over financial fit, stewardship of acquired legacy
- **MA12-01 AI-Powered Deal Sourcing & Valuation** — Predictive target identification, automated due diligence, NLP contract analysis, real-time synergy modeling
- **Master Formula MAE** = (Strategic_Fit × Valuation_Accuracy × Integration_Execution × Cultural_Alignment × Synergy_Realization) / (Overpayment × Hubris × Integration_Failure × Cultural_Clash × Short_Termism)
