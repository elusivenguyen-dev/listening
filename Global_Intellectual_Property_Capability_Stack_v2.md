## Điểm nổi bật

- **IP01-01 Creative Intuition & Idea Protection Instinct** — Somatic awareness of idea value, embodied creativity protection, intuitive IP sensing, personal intellectual stewardship
- **IP03-01 IP Fundamentals & Portfolio Architecture** — Patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets, IP valuation, freedom-to-operate (FTO), prior art search, prosecution strategy
- **IP06-01 Board-Level IP Strategy & Competitive Moat** — IP as strategic asset, patent thickets, defensive vs. offensive IP, IP monetization, licensing strategy, IP committee governance
- **IP08-01 Global IP Enforcement & Cross-Border Litigation** — Patent troll defense, international arbitration, WIPO procedures, standard-essential patents (SEP), FRAND licensing, IP in emerging markets
- **IP11-01 IP as Legacy — Knowledge Stewardship** — Open source philosophy, creative commons, knowledge as commons, balancing protection and sharing, IP for human flourishing
- **IP12-01 AI-Generated IP & Autonomous Innovation Protection** — AI inventorship debate, generative IP creation, automated patent drafting, AI prior art analysis, predictive IP landscape mapping
- **Master Formula IPE** = (Creativity × Protection × Enforcement × Monetization × Strategic_Alignment × Freedom_to_Operate) / (Overprotection × Patent_Thickets × Litigation_Abuse × Stagnation × Open_Source_Paranoia)
