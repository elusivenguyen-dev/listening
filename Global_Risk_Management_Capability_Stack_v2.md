## Điểm nổi bật

- **RM01-01 Somatic Risk Sensing** — Body-based intuition for danger, gut feeling calibration, nervous system threat detection, embodied prudence
- **RM03-01 Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) Frameworks** — COSO, ISO 31000, risk appetite, risk taxonomy, heat maps, KRIs, scenario analysis, stress testing
- **RM06-01 Board-Level Risk Governance & CRO Strategy** — Risk committee oversight, three lines of defense, risk culture, emerging risk identification, board risk literacy
- **RM08-01 Black Swan, Tail Risk & Complex Adaptive Systems** — Taleb's antifragility, extreme event modeling, systemic risk, interconnected risk cascades, geopolitical risk intelligence
- **RM11-01 The Wise Steward — Risk as Responsibility** — Precautionary principle, intergenerational risk, stewardship of the unknown, humility in the face of uncertainty
- **RM12-01 AI-Powered Predictive Risk Intelligence** — Real-time risk sensing, predictive risk modeling, autonomous risk response, digital twin stress testing, AI red teaming
- **Master Formula RME** = (Identification × Assessment × Mitigation × Monitoring × Resilience × Opportunity_Capture) / (Blindness × Complacency × Silo_Risk × Short_Termism × Overconfidence × Reactive_Only)
