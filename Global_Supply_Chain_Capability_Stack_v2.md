## Điểm nổi bật

- **SC01-01 Personal Resource Scarcity Awareness** — Understanding flow, inventory, waste in daily life; lean thinking as mindset
- **SC03-01 Supply Chain Operations & Logistics Fundamentals** — SCOR model, lean Six Sigma, JIT, EOQ, warehouse design, last-mile delivery
- **SC06-01 Global Supply Chain Strategy & Reshoring Decisions** — Make vs. buy, nearshoring, friend-shoring, geopolitical risk in sourcing
- **SC08-01 Multi-Tier Visibility & Supply Chain Finance** — Blockchain traceability, supplier financing, conflict minerals, forced labor auditing
- **SC11-01 The Web of Interdependence — Supply Chain as Philosophy** — No entity exists alone, ripple effects of decisions, stewardship across tiers
- **SC12-01 Self-Healing Autonomous Supply Networks** — AI demand sensing, autonomous procurement, predictive disruption mitigation, digital twin supply chains
- **Master Formula SCE** = (Visibility × Resilience × Efficiency × Sustainability × Agility × Collaboration) / (Fragility × Opacity × Waste × Single_Sourcing × Geopolitical_Blindness)
