## Executive Summary

The Global Crisis Management (CZM) capability stack provides CEOs with a comprehensive 12-tier architecture for anticipating, preparing for, responding to, and recovering from crises across global operations. This capability transcends traditional crisis management to strategic crisis architecture — transforming crises from existential threats into opportunities for organizational evolution, stakeholder trust deepening, and competitive differentiation.

**Strategic Imperative:** In an era of polycrisis, where financial, geopolitical, environmental, cyber, and reputational crises intersect and amplify, the CEO who masters crisis management does not merely survive — they emerge stronger, more trusted, and more resilient than before.
