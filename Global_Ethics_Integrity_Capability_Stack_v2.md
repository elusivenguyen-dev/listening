## Master Formula — Ethics & Integrity Effectiveness (ETE)

```
ETE = (Consistency × Courage × Transparency × Accountability × Humility) / (Rationalization × Hypocrisy × Complacency × Arrogance)

Where:
- Consistency = Alignment of words and actions (0-10)
- Courage = Willingness to do right when costly (0-10)
- Transparency = Openness about decisions and motives (0-10)
- Accountability = Owning mistakes and consequences (0-10)
- Humility = Recognizing moral limits and fallibility (0-10)
- Rationalization = Justifying questionable behavior (inverse, 0-10)
- Hypocrisy = Saying one thing, doing another (inverse, 0-10)
- Complacency = Assuming ethics is "handled" (inverse, 0-10)
- Arrogance = Moral superiority over others (inverse, 0-10)

Target ETE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
