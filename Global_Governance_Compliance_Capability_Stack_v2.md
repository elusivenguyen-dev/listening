## Điểm nổi bật

- **GC01-01 Personal Integrity & Internal Governance** — Self-regulation as governance foundation, embodied accountability, personal code of conduct, congruence between values and actions
- **GC03-01 Corporate Governance Frameworks & Compliance Systems** — OECD principles, UK Corporate Governance Code, SOX, GDPR, AML/KYC, internal controls, whistleblower programs
- **GC06-01 Board Governance Excellence & Audit Committee Leadership** — Board effectiveness evaluation, independent director quality, audit committee oversight, related-party transaction governance, executive compensation governance
- **GC08-01 Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance & Regulatory Navigation** — Cross-border regulatory complexity, sanctions compliance, data sovereignty, tax transparency, anti-bribery (FCPA, UK Bribery Act), trade compliance
- **GC11-01 Governance as Sacred Trust — Stewardship of Institution** — Governance beyond compliance, fiduciary duty as sacred obligation, institutional memory, governance as legacy, the board as guardian of purpose
- **GC12-01 AI Governance & Autonomous Compliance** — AI ethics governance, algorithmic auditing, autonomous regulatory monitoring, predictive compliance, self-reporting systems, real-time regulatory adaptation
- **Master Formula GCE** = (Transparency × Accountability × Independence × Effectiveness × Compliance_Culture × Stakeholder_Alignment) / (Bureaucracy × Box_Ticking × Capture × Short_Termism × Silo_Governance × Regulatory_Arbitrage)
