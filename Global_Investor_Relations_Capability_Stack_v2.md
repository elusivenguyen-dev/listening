## Điểm nổi bật

- **IR01-01 Authentic Investor Presence** — Embodied credibility, somatic trustworthiness, genuine connection with capital providers, personal conviction in investor communication
- **IR03-01 IR Fundamentals & Disclosure Excellence** — Reg FD, earnings calls, 10-K/10-Q mastery, investor day design, shareholder letters, ESG disclosure, proxy statements, IR website optimization
- **IR06-01 C-Suite IR Strategy & Activist Defense** — CEO-CFO investor engagement, shareholder base management, activist shareholder preparation, institutional investor targeting, retail investor relations
- **IR08-01 Global IR & Cross-Border Investor Engagement** — Emerging market investor access, sovereign wealth dialogue, hedge fund relations, family office engagement, ESG investor targeting, retail shareholder activism
- **IR11-01 The Covenant with Capital — Trust Over Transaction** — Long-term investor partnership, transparency as virtue, investor relations as stewardship, honest communication over spin, building generational investor trust
- **IR12-01 AI-Powered Investor Intelligence & Personalized Engagement** — Predictive investor sentiment, automated earnings preparation, personalized investor communication at scale, real-time shareholder analytics, AI-driven activist early warning
- **Master Formula IRE** = (Transparency × Consistency × Accessibility × Responsiveness × Strategic_Narrative × Trust) / (Spin × Inconsistency × Inaccessibility × Delay × Short_Term_Flattery × Information_Asymmetry)
