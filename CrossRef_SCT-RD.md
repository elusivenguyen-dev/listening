## Integration Philosophy

R&D without Scenario Thinking is **inventing for a single assumed future**. Scenario Thinking without R&D is **imagining futures without the capability to create them**. The SCT-RD integration ensures that research is **future-relevant** and that scenarios guide **technology roadmaps, research prioritization, and innovation timing**.

> *"Research is not about discovering what is. It is about creating what could be."*
