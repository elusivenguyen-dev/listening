# PHẦN 4: INFORMATION MASTERY INDEX (IMI) v3.0

## 4.1 Formula

```
Information Mastery Index (IMI) = 

    ╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
    ║  4√(LE × RE × WE × SE)  ← Geometric Mean of 4 Capabilities      ║
    ║  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────    ║
    ║                    [Input_Harmonization]                          ║
    ║                                                                   ║
    ║  × [Processing_Depth × Processing_Speed]                        ║
    ║                                                                   ║
    ║  × [Output_Harmonization]                                       ║
    ║                                                                   ║
    ║  × [AI_Amplification_Factor]                                      ║
    ║                                                                   ║
    ║  × [Wisdom_Integration_Multiplier]                                ║
    ║                                                                   ║
    ║  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────    ║
    ║  [Cognitive_Load + Time_Investment + Bias_Exposure]               ║
    ╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Component Definitions:

LE (Listening Effectiveness) = 
    [Comprehension × Retention × Application] / [Distraction × Fatigue]

RE (Reading Effectiveness) = 
    [Speed × Comprehension × Retention × Application] / [Cognitive_Load + Time + Bias]

WE (Writing Effectiveness) = 
    [Clarity × Impact × Persuasion × Originality × Speed] / [Cognitive_Load + Time + Revisions + Bias]

SE (Speaking Effectiveness) = 
    [Clarity × Engagement × Persuasion × Adaptability × Vocal_Quality] / [Prep_Time + Anxiety + Bias + Strain]

Input_Harmonization = 1 + (Listening_Reading_Sync × 0.3)
Output_Harmonization = 1 + (Writing_Speaking_Sync × 0.3)

Processing_Depth = Analysis + Synthesis + Evaluation + Creation (0-4 scale, each 0-1)
Processing_Speed = Time_to_insight / Time_to_decision (0-1 scale)

AI_Amplification_Factor = 1 + (AI_tools_used_effectively × 0.5)
Wisdom_Integration_Multiplier = 1 + (philosophical_frameworks_applied × 0.3)

Cognitive_Load = Mental effort / capacity (0-1, inverted in denominator)
Time_Investment = Hours / optimal hours (0-1, inverted)
Bias_Exposure = Number of unchecked biases detected (0-1, inverted)
```
