## Master Formula — Negotiation Effectiveness (NE)

```
NE = (Preparation × Relationship × Creativity × Assertiveness) / (Reactivity × Desperation × Win_At_All_Costs)

Where:
- Preparation = Prep time / Deal complexity (0-10)
- Relationship = Trust level with counterpart (0-10)
- Creativity = Novel options generated (0-10)
- Assertiveness = Clear advocacy of interests (0-10)
- Reactivity = Emotional response to tactics (inverse, 0-10)
- Desperation = Neediness signals (inverse, 0-10)
- Win_At_All_Costs = Relationship damage for margin (inverse, 0-10)

Target NE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
