## Master Formula — Cultural Intelligence Effectiveness (CIE)

```
CIE = (Self_Awareness × Knowledge × Adaptation × Empathy × Humility) / (Ethnocentrism × Stereotyping × Arrogance × Complacency)

Where:
- Self_Awareness = Understanding own cultural programming (0-10)
- Knowledge = Understanding of other cultures (0-10)
- Adaptation = Ability to adjust behavior appropriately (0-10)
- Empathy = Emotional connection across cultures (0-10)
- Humility = Willingness to learn and be corrected (0-10)
- Ethnocentrism = Assuming own culture is superior (inverse, 0-10)
- Stereotyping = Applying generalizations to individuals (inverse, 0-10)
- Arrogance = Assuming you understand without learning (inverse, 0-10)
- Complacency = Stopping learning about cultures (inverse, 0-10)

Target CIE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
