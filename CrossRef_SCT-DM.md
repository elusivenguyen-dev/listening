## Integration Philosophy

Every decision is a bet on the future. Without Scenario Thinking, Decision Making operates on **implicit, unexamined assumptions about that future**. Without Decision Making, Scenario Thinking produces **elegant but inert futures**. The SCT-DM integration makes every decision **explicitly future-aware** and every scenario **explicitly decision-relevant**.

> *"The quality of a decision is determined by the quality of the futures it considers."*

## Joint Programs (12 Integrated Programs)

### SCT-DM-01: Scenario-Weighted Decision Tree
**Description:** Build decision trees where each branch is weighted by scenario probability and emotional impact.  
**Method:** Decision tree × scenario probability × affective forecasting  
**Output:** Scenario-optimized decision map

### SCT-DM-02: Multi-Future Option Evaluation
**Description:** Evaluate strategic options against multiple futures simultaneously.  
**Method:** Option × scenario matrix with robustness scoring  
**Output:** Option robustness ranking

### SCT-DM-03: Scenario-Based Pre-Decision Review
**Description:** Mandatory scenario review before major decisions (>threshold).  
**Method:** Decision threshold × scenario checklist  
**Output:** Pre-decision scenario certificate

### SCT-DM-04: Adaptive Decision Protocols
**Description:** Define decision rules that change based on which scenario is unfolding.  
**Method:** If-then decision rules × scenario indicators  
**Output:** Adaptive decision playbook

### SCT-DM-05: Decision Pre-Mortem with Scenarios
**Description:** Imagine decision failure in each scenario and work backward to prevent it.  
**Method:** Pre-mortem × scenario set  
**Output:** Scenario-aware failure prevention plan

### SCT-DM-06: Scenario-Based Capital Allocation
**Description:** Allocate capital across bets based on scenario robustness and probability.  
**Method:** Portfolio theory × scenario weights  
**Output:** Scenario-optimized capital allocation

### SCT-DM-07: Real-Time Decision Triggers
**Description:** Automated alerts when scenario indicators trigger pre-defined decision points.  
**Method:** Scenario dashboard × decision trigger engine  
**Output:** Real-time decision support system

### SCT-DM-08: Group Decision Scenario Facilitation
**Description:** Use scenarios to reduce groupthink and expand decision frames in team decisions.  
**Method:** Scenario workshop × group decision protocol  
**Output:** Group decision quality improvement

### SCT-DM-09: Emotional Decision Calibration
**Description:** Calibrate emotional responses to scenario outcomes for better decision quality.  
**Method:** Affective forecasting × decision calibration  
**Output:** Emotionally calibrated decision maker

### SCT-DM-10: Black Swan Decision Preparedness
**Description:** Build decision reserves and options for extreme scenario events.  
**Method:** Black Swan scenarios × optionality strategy  
**Output:** Extreme event decision readiness

### SCT-DM-11: Decision Legacy Analysis
**Description:** Analyze how today's decisions will be viewed from future scenario perspectives.  
**Method:** Temporal empathy × decision evaluation  
**Output:** Future-proofed decision rationale

### SCT-DM-12: AI-Augmented Scenario Decision Support
**Description:** AI system that models millions of scenario-decision combinations.  
**Method:** ML simulation × decision optimization  
**Output:** AI decision recommendation engine
