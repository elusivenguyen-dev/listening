## Điểm nổi bật

- **PM01-01 Customer Empathy as Instinct** — Embodied user understanding, intuition for unmet needs, sensory product experience, visceral design sense
- **PM03-01 Product Management Frameworks** — Lean startup, jobs-to-be-done, MVP, product-market fit metrics, OKRs, agile/Scrum for product teams
- **PM06-01 Product Portfolio Strategy** — BCG for products, platform strategy, SKU rationalization, product line architecture, innovation portfolio management
- **PM08-01 Platform Product & Ecosystem Governance** — API design, developer experience, two-sided markets, network effects, platform economics, marketplace liquidity
- **PM11-01 Product as Philosophy** — Craftsmanship, long-term product integrity, anti-consumerism design, products that age well, sustainable product thinking
- **PM12-01 AI-Native Product Autonomy** — Self-improving products, generative feature creation, predictive churn, autonomous A/B testing at scale, agentic product loops
- **Master Formula PME** = (Customer_Value × Market_Fit × Execution_Speed × Innovation_Depth × Business_Model_Alignment) / (Feature_Creep × Vanity_Metrics × Silo_Development × Technical_Debt × Market_Misfit)
