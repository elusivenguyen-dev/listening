# Global Scenario Thinking Capability Stack v2.0 (SCTCS v2.0)

**Capability Code:** `SCT`  
**Capability Name:** Scenario Thinking (Tư duy kịch bản)  
**Classification:** Meta-capability độc lập + Cross-cutting enabler  
**Version:** v2.0  
**Programs:** 90 (12 Tiers × 10 Programs)  
**Integration:** CEO Capability Matrix v32.0 — Capability thứ 89

---

## Master Formula — SCTE (Scenario Thinking Effectiveness)

```
SCTE = (Plausibility × Diversity × Actionability × Temporal_Depth × Stakeholder_Richness × Update_Frequency × AI_Augmentation) 
       / (Prediction_Illusion × Single_Future_Bias × Paralysis × Outdated_Scenarios × Echo_Chamber × Complexity_Overwhelm × Inaction)
```

**Target Scores:**
- CEO Practitioner: SCTE > 750
- Enterprise Foresight Function: SCTE > 1500
- AI-Augmented Foresight System: SCTE > 3000
