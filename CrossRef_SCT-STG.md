## Integration Philosophy

Strategic Thinking without Scenario Thinking is **optimism bias dressed as strategy**. Scenario Thinking without Strategic Thinking is **beautiful fiction without action**. The SCT-STG integration ensures that strategy is both **visionary and robust** — built on multiple validated futures rather than a single preferred future.

> *"Strategy is not about predicting the future. It is about preparing for multiple futures."*

## Joint Programs (12 Integrated Programs)

### SCT-STG-01: Scenario-Strategy Translation Engine
**Description:** Systematic process to convert scenario insights into strategic options, strategic bets, and portfolio adjustments.  
**Method:** Scenario-to-strategy matrix × strategic option evaluation  
**Output:** Strategic option portfolio ranked by scenario robustness

### SCT-STG-02: Adaptive Strategy Governance
**Description:** Governance model where strategy is reviewed and adjusted based on scenario indicator dashboards.  
**Method:** Quarterly scenario-strategy board review  
**Output:** Living strategy document with scenario triggers

### SCT-STG-03: Future-Back Strategic Planning
**Description:** Start from preferred future scenario and work backward to define strategic milestones and investments.  
**Method:** Backcasting × strategic planning cycle  
**Output:** Future-back strategic roadmap with investment timeline

### SCT-STG-04: Strategic Wind Tunneling
**Description:** Test all strategic initiatives against full scenario set before approval.  
**Method:** Initiative × scenario stress test matrix  
**Output:** Strategy robustness scorecard

### SCT-STG-05: Three Horizons Portfolio Management
**Description:** Manage strategic portfolio across H1 (current), H2 (transitional), H3 (transformative) using scenario insights.  
**Method:** Three Horizons × scenario probability mapping  
**Output:** Balanced innovation-strategy portfolio

### SCT-STG-06: Scenario-Based Competitive Strategy
**Description:** Use competitor scenario wargaming to inform competitive positioning strategy.  
**Method:** Wargaming × competitive strategy framework  
**Output:** Competitor-aware strategic positioning

### SCT-STG-07: Strategic Early Warning System
**Description:** Define leading indicators from scenarios that trigger strategic pivots.  
**Method:** Scenario indicators × strategic trigger protocols  
**Output:** Automated strategic alert system

### SCT-STG-08: Board Scenario-Strategy Education
**Description:** Train board members to think strategically through scenarios.  
**Method:** Board scenario workshops × strategic governance training  
**Output:** Board futures literacy certification

### SCT-STG-09: Anti-Fragile Strategy Design
**Description:** Design strategies that gain from volatility using Taleb-inspired scenario methods.  
**Method:** Black Swan scenarios × optionality strategy  
**Output:** Anti-fragile strategic architecture

### SCT-STG-10: Geopolitical Strategy Integration
**Description:** Embed geopolitical scenario analysis into global strategic planning.  
**Method:** Geopolitical scenarios × market entry/exit strategy  
**Output:** Geopolitically resilient global strategy

### SCT-STG-11: Strategic Foresight Culture
**Description:** Build organizational culture where every strategic conversation includes future scenarios.  
**Method:** Culture change × foresight capability building  
**Output:** Future-ready strategic culture

### SCT-STG-12: AI-Augmented Strategic Foresight
**Description:** Use AI to continuously scan, model, and update strategic scenarios.  
**Method:** AI scanning × strategic scenario modeling
