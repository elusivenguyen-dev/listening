## Capability Overview

**Code:** GRD  
**Name:** Global Rule Designer  
**Domain:** Global Standard Architecture, Policy Innovation & Regulatory Design  
**Purpose:** Equip CEOs with the ultimate meta-capability to design, architect, and influence the global rules that govern markets, industries, technologies, and societies — moving from rule-follower to rule-maker.

**Capability Thesis:** The highest form of leadership is not winning within the rules. It is designing the rules themselves. The Global Rule Designer does not adapt to the environment; they architect the environment in which all others must adapt.
