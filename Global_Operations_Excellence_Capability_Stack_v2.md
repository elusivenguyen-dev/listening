## Điểm nổi bật

- **OE01-01 Personal Operational Discipline** — Kaizen mindset in daily life, attention to detail as meditation, process thinking as second nature, personal 5S
- **OE03-01 Lean, Six Sigma & TQM Fundamentals** — DMAIC, value stream mapping, 7 wastes, poka-yoke, standard work, total productive maintenance (TPM), quality circles
- **OE06-01 Operations Strategy & Competitive Operations** — Operations as competitive weapon, cost leadership vs. differentiation through operations, operations strategy alignment
- **OE08-01 Advanced Manufacturing & Industry 4.0** — Smart factories, IoT operations, predictive maintenance, digital thread, additive manufacturing, mass customization
- **OE11-01 The Craftsman's Way — Mastery Through Repetition** — Shokunin spirit, 10,000 hours with intention, operational excellence as spiritual practice, beauty in process
- **OE12-01 Autonomous Operations & Self-Optimizing Systems** — Lights-out manufacturing, AI-driven process optimization, self-correcting supply chains, real-time OEE maximization, predictive quality control
- **Master Formula OEE** = (Quality × Speed × Cost_Efficiency × Flexibility × Reliability × Safety) / (Waste × Variability × Bottlenecks × Complacency × Short_Cuts × Silo_Operations)
