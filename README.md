## 📐 Ecosystem Architecture

### The 12-Tier Progressive Mastery Model

Every capability follows the same 12-tier architecture, ensuring systematic, progressive skill development:

| Tier | Name | Mastery Level | Scope | Focus |
|------|------|--------------|-------|-------|
| **1** | Foundation | Awareness | Individual | Basic concepts & recognition |
| **2** | Operational | Application | Team | Tools & techniques |
| **3** | Tactical | Execution | Department | Integration & mapping |
| **4** | Strategic | Synthesis | Division | Multi-domain alignment |
| **5** | Advanced | Analysis | Business Unit | Causal inference & modeling |
| **6** | Expert | AI-Augmented | Enterprise | Machine learning & automation |
| **7** | Master | Network Scale | Enterprise+ | Ecosystem & platform thinking |
| **8** | Architect | Intervention | Multi-Enterprise | Design & transformation |
| **9** | Visionary | Predictive | Ecosystem | Foresight & generative design |
| **10** | Transformative | Self-Organizing | Ecosystem | Autopoiesis & emergence |
| **11** | Transcendent | Philosophical | Planetary | Meta-systems & consciousness |
| **12** | Omega | Universal | Cosmic | Self-transcending & immortal |

**Each Tier contains 10 Programs** → 120 Programs per Capability.

## 🚀 How to Use This Ecosystem

### For Individual Learners
1. **Assess** your current tier across capabilities using the baseline audits in Tier 1
2. **Choose** a Learning Path based on your role and aspirations
3. **Progress** through tiers sequentially — each tier builds on the previous
4. **Integrate** across capabilities — the real power is at the intersections
5. **Reflect** using the mantras and philosophy in Tiers 11–12

### For Organizations
1. **Map** your leadership team against the 149 capabilities
2. **Identify** capability gaps at the enterprise level
3. **Design** custom learning journeys for different leadership levels
4. **Measure** progress using the tier completion metrics
5. **Evolve** the ecosystem itself — add capabilities as your industry demands

### For Educators & Coaches
1. Use individual programs as curriculum modules
2. Combine programs from multiple capabilities for integrated case studies
3. Use Tier 12 programs as capstone projects for executive programs
4. Reference the ecosystem as a comprehensive competency framework
