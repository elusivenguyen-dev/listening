## Điểm nổi bật

- **CR01-01 Somatic De-escalation** — Body-based regulation before verbal engagement, polyvagal theory in conflict
- **CR03-01 Interest-Based Negotiation & Mediation Models** — Fisher/Ury principled negotiation, transformative mediation, Harvard model
- **CR06-01 Boardroom & C-Suite Conflict Navigation** — Power dynamics, succession conflict, founder-CEO tension, fiduciary duty clashes
- **CR08-01 Cross-Cultural & Multi-Party Conflict Systems** — High-context vs. low-context resolution, tribal conflict, international arbitration
- **CR11-01 Taoist & Stoic Conflict Wisdom** — Wu wei in confrontation, Marcus Aurelius on anger, turning conflict into harmony
- **CR12-01 AI-Powered Early Warning & De-escalation** — Sentiment prediction, conversational pattern analysis, real-time coaching during heated exchanges
- **Master Formula CRE** = (Empathy × Neutrality × Creativity × Legitimacy × Relationship_Preservation) / (Escalation × Zero_Sum × Avoidance × Power_Abuse × Retaliation)
