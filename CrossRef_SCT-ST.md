## Integration Philosophy

Scenario Thinking without Systems Thinking produces **linear futures** that ignore feedback loops, delays, and emergent behavior. Systems Thinking without Scenario Thinking produces **deep understanding of the present** but no ability to imagine alternative futures. Together, they create **dynamic scenario models** that capture complexity, emergence, and non-linearity.

> *"The future is not a line. It is a system of interacting possibilities."*
