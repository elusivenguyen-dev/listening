## Điểm nổi bật

- **BM01-01 Personal Brand Authenticity & Embodied Presence** — Congruence between inner values and outer expression, somatic brand alignment, personal magnetism
- **BM03-01 Brand Strategy & Positioning Fundamentals** — Aaker brand equity, Keller CBBE, positioning statement, brand architecture, brand extension, co-branding
- **BM06-01 Enterprise Brand Portfolio Strategy** — House of brands vs. branded house, brand valuation, M&A brand integration, employer branding, brand as strategic asset
- **BM08-01 Global Brand Localization & Cultural Semiotics** — Glocalization, semiotic analysis, cultural brand meaning, transcreation, brand activism, reputation management in polarized world
- **BM11-01 Brand as Covenant — Long-Term Trust & Legacy** — Brand as promise kept across generations, anti-hype branding, patience in brand building, brand stewardship over brand exploitation
- **BM12-01 AI-Dynamic Brand & Real-Time Reputation Engine** — Generative brand identity, real-time brand sentiment optimization, AI brand voice, predictive reputation management, autonomous brand adaptation
- **Master Formula BME** = (Authenticity × Differentiation × Consistency × Relevance × Trust × Emotional_Resonance) / (Inauthenticity × Hype × Inconsistency × Obsolescence × Short_Term_Gimmicks)
