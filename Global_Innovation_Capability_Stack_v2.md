## Master Formula — Innovation Effectiveness (IE)

```
IE = (Novelty × Value × Feasibility × Speed) / (Rework × Market_Misfit × Execution_Gap)

Where:
- Novelty = Degree of newness (0-10)
- Value = Customer/problem fit (0-10)
- Feasibility = Technical/business viability (0-10)
- Speed = Time from idea to market (0-10)
- Rework = Iterations due to poor initial concept (inverse, 0-10)
- Market_Misfit = Lack of market need (inverse, 0-10)
- Execution_Gap = Failure to execute (inverse, 0-10)

Target IE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
