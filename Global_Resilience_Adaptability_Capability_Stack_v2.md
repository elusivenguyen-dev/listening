## Master Formula — Resilience & Adaptability Effectiveness (RAE)

```
RAE = (Recovery_Speed × Adaptation_Quality × Growth_From_Adversity × Support_Leverage) / (Fragility × Rigidity × Avoidance × Denial)

Where:
- Recovery_Speed = Time to baseline after setback (inverse, 0-10)
- Adaptation_Quality = Effectiveness of behavioral change (0-10)
- Growth_From_Adversity = Learning and strengthening post-crisis (0-10)
- Support_Leverage = Using support systems effectively (0-10)
- Fragility = Susceptibility to breakdown (inverse, 0-10)
- Rigidity = Inability to change approach (inverse, 0-10)
- Avoidance = Refusing to face challenges (inverse, 0-10)
- Denial = Ignoring problems until crisis (inverse, 0-10)

Target RAE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
