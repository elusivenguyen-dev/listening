## Điểm nổi bật

- **DT01-01 Embodied Empathy** — Somatic awareness, mirror neurons, emotional attunement before cognitive analysis
- **DT03-01 IDEO Deep Dive & Stanford d.school** — Empathize → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test, with iteration loops
- **DT06-01 Design-Led Business Strategy** — Design as competitive moat, design maturity model, C-suite design literacy
- **DT08-01 Service Design & Systems Thinking Integration** — Touchpoint mapping, service blueprints, journey orchestration
- **DT11-01 Wabi-Sabi & Imperfect Design Wisdom** — Beauty in imperfection, continuous iteration, anti-perfectionism
- **DT12-01 Generative AI Co-Design** — AI as creative partner, prompt-driven prototyping, synthetic user research
- **Master Formula DTE** = (Empathy × Creativity × Iteration_Speed × Human_Centeredness) / (Solutionism × Confirmation_Bias × Rigidity × Untested_Assumptions)
