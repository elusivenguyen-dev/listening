## Master Formula — Physical Fitness Effectiveness (PFE)

```
PFE = (Strength × Endurance × Mobility × Recovery × Consistency) / (Injury × Burnout × Inconsistency)

Where:
- Strength = Relative strength benchmarks (0-10)
- Endurance = Cardiovascular capacity (0-10)
- Mobility = Joint range + movement quality (0-10)
- Recovery = Sleep + HRV + fatigue management (0-10)
- Consistency = Days active / Total days (0-10)
- Injury = Downtime due to injury (inverse, 0-10)
- Burnout = Overtraining signs (inverse, 0-10)
- Inconsistency = Gap days >3 in a row (inverse, 0-10)

Target PFE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
