- **SE11-01 The Circle of Stakeholders — Interconnected Responsibility** — All beings as stakeholders, seventh generation thinking, reciprocity as cosmic law, stakeholder engagement as spiritual practice
- **SE12-01 AI-Powered Stakeholder Intelligence & Predictive Engagement** — Real-time sentiment monitoring across all stakeholder groups, predictive conflict detection, automated personalized engagement at scale
