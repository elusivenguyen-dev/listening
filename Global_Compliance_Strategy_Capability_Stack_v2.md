## Executive Summary

The Global Compliance Strategy (CPS) capability stack provides CEOs with a comprehensive 12-tier architecture for building world-class compliance programs that transcend mere regulatory adherence to become strategic competitive advantages. This capability integrates legal, ethical, regulatory, and cultural compliance into a unified governance framework that protects the enterprise while enabling bold strategic moves.

**Strategic Imperative:** In an era of escalating regulatory complexity, ethical consumerism, and stakeholder capitalism, compliance is no longer a cost center — it is a license to operate, a trust accelerator, and a strategic differentiator.
