## Master Formula — Emotional Intelligence Effectiveness (EIE)

```
EIE = (Self_Awareness × Self_Regulation × Empathy × Social_Skill) / (Suppression × Reactivity × Blind_Spots)

Where:
- Self_Awareness = Emotion identification accuracy (0-10)
- Self_Regulation = Recovery time from trigger (inverse, 0-10)
- Empathy = Perspective-taking quality (0-10)
- Social_Skill = Relationship management effectiveness (0-10)
- Suppression = Tendency to hide emotions (inverse, 0-10)
- Reactivity = Impulsive emotional response (inverse, 0-10)
- Blind_Spots = Unrecognized emotional patterns (inverse, 0-10)

Target EIE: Personal > 5.0 | Manager > 7.0 | Executive > 8.5 | CEO > 9.0
```
