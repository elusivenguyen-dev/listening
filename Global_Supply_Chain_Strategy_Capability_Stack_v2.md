## Integration Philosophy

**Supply Chain Strategy × CEO Leadership**  
Supply chain is the nervous system of the enterprise. Every supplier is a partner in value creation. Every logistics node is a strategic position. Every inventory decision is a bet on demand. A CEO with Supply Chain Strategy mastery doesn't optimize costs—they architect resilient networks, orchestrate global ecosystems, and turn supply chain into a competitive weapon that delivers speed, quality, and sustainability.

**Synergy Thesis:**  
> *"Supply chain is not merely about moving goods. It is about moving value, moving trust, moving sustainability, and moving competitive advantage through a network of relationships, technologies, and capabilities. Master supply chain, and you master the physical manifestation of strategy."*
