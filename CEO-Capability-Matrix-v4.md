## Executive Intelligence Index (EI) v4.0

### Formula

```
EI = 6√(LE × RE × WE × SE × DME × STE) × PD × AI × WI

Where:
- LE = Listening Effectiveness (0-10)
- RE = Reading Effectiveness (0-10)
- WE = Writing Effectiveness (0-10)
- SE = Speaking Effectiveness (0-10)
- DME = Decision Making Effectiveness (0-10)
- STE = Systems Thinking Effectiveness (0-10)
- PD = Processing Depth (0-10) — integration quality across capabilities
- AI = AI Amplification (0-10) — AI augmentation level
- WI = Wisdom Integration (0-10) — long-term, ethical, institutional integration

Target:
- Manager: EI > 5.0
- Director: EI > 8.0
- VP: EI > 12.0
- CEO: EI > 20.0
- Institution Builder: EI > 35.0
- AI-Augmented Leader: EI > 50.0
```
